Lasagne

Lasagne Verdi al Forno

This lasagne verde cannot be beaten!

Ingredients

Pasta:

  • 5 ounces spinach - rinsed, stemmed and dried
  • 5/8 cup semolina flour
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 1/2 cups of all-purpose flour

Ragu:

  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 carrot, diced
  • 1 stalk celery, diced
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 slices bacon, diced
  • 3 1/3 ounces lean ground pork
  • 11⁄2

Steps

  1. Make pasta
  2. Make ragu
  3. Make bechamel
  4. Cook pasta
  5. Finish ragu
  6. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F
  7. Assemble lasagna
  8. Bake in preheated oven
